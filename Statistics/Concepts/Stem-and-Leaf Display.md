## A stem-and-leaf display provides valuable information:
• **Identification of a typical or representative value:** Helps determine the central tendency of the data.
• **Extent of spread about the typical value:** Shows how data values are dispersed around the center.
• **Presence of any gaps in the data:** Highlights areas where data points are missing or sparse.
• **Extent of symmetry in the distribution of values:** Reveals whether the data is symmetrical or skewed.
• **Number and locations of peaks:** Identifies modes or clusters within the data.
• **Presence of any outliers:** Detects values significantly distant from the rest of the data.

### Trimmed mean
a compromise between x and $\bar{x}$. A 10% trimmed mean, for example, would be computed by eliminating the smallest 10% and the largest 10% of the sample and then averaging what remains.
## Deficiency: inappropriate measure of center under some circumstances

^9196ac

- Its value can be greatly affected by the presence of even a single outlier (unusually large or small observation). ^6edcc6

## Notation:
- **population mean**:  Greek letter $\mu$
- **sample mean:** $\bar{x}$ (x bar)

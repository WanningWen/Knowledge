> [!important] [[Schizophrenia]] symptoms present for at least one month but _not_ longer than six months.
> “intermediate” disorder between schizophrenia and brief psychotic disorder
> [[Schizophrenia]]: at least six months
> [[brief psychotic disorder]]: _less_ than one month.
## Clinical Presentation
- duration: 1-6 months
- not essential to have impaired functioning
- short major mood episodes (depressive or [[Manic episode|manic]]), otherwise better diagnosed as [[Schizoaffective Disorder]] (APA, 2022).
## Epidemiology/ Prevalence
- Approximately 2/3 of individuals are initially diagnosed with schizophreniform disorder, but changed to [[Schizophrenia]] after 6 months (APA, 2022).

> [!important] the written record of the agreements and decisions between the person and the other members of the treatment team, along with other important people in the individual’s life. 

#### Steps with [[Person-Centered Care]]
- start with recognition of the individual’s strengths and personal assets and help the person to identify his or her own unique recovery goal. 
- The next step is to identify the ==barriers to that recovery vision== that arise from:
	- their mental health problems
	- general health problems
	- substance misuse
	- other needs or concerns.

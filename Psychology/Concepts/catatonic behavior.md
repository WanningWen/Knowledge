> [!important] the decreased or complete lack of reactivity to the environment
### catatonic negativism
resistance to instruction
### catatonic - _mutism_ or _stupor_
complete lack of verbal and motor responses
- remains totally motionless and silent for long periods of time
### catatonic _rigidity_
rigid upright posture for long periods of time, resist others helping them move
### catatonic _posturing_
holding odd, awkward postures for long periods)
### - _catatonic excitement_
hyperactivity of motor behavior, in a seemingly excited or delirious way
-​often waving their arms or legs.

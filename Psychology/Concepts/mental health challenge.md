> - there's a major change in a person's **thinking**, **feeling**, or **acting** 
> - this change interferes with the person's ability to live their life
> - interference does not go away quickly and lasts longer than typical emotions/reactions

- encompasses  signs and symptoms of
	- early
	- worsening
	- crisis

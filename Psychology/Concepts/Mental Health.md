# Definition:
>[!note] World Mental Health Organization
>Mental health is a state of well-being in which an individual:
>- realizes their own abilities
>- can cope with normal stresses of life
>- can work productively
>- contribute to their community

## Backgrounds and Enviornmental Impact

- who we go for support
- what we think causes good and bad health
- what health means to us

## Mental health deaths:
[[Healing Our path from mental illness to mental health]]
- over 47,000 suicide deaths in the U.S. each year, the equivalent of a mass shooting of 129 people each day, every day.
- a suicide every 11 minutes. 
	- three times more suicides than homicides each year
- suicide as a cause of medical mortality surpasses breast cancer, prostate cancer, and AIDS.

At least two thirds, some would say 90 percent, of suicides result from depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, or one of the other categories of mental illness.

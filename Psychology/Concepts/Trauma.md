> A normal response to an abnormal event that causes disruption of an equilibrium
### Clues and Signs of Unresolved Trauma
clues:
- a history of childhood invalidation, harm or neglect
- a history of frightening, frightened or non-responsive significant others
- difficulties with identifying, tolerating or regulating strong emotions in oneself and others
- problems in recognizing or accepting help from others, or the opposite (i.e., being very dependent, demanding and needy)
Signs
- becomes less coherent when speaking of a relationship or frightening interpersonal experience 
	- language becomes unclear
	- disconnected sentences 
	- misassumptions regarding the therapist knowing about people or events
- reports events in a minimizing way without emotion
- becomes so overwhelmed with emotion that they can’t speak
- lacks memories or is unable to describe experiences or relationships in ways that are understandable
- dissociates with a sudden shift in behaviour or change in tone, voice or manner

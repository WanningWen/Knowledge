> [!NOTE] Analyst silently listens for the unconscious material of patient's free associations and interpret the early childhood origins of their problems 
> once unconscious material is made conscious -> patient could be cured

### classical techniques: 
interpretation of **free association**, **dreams**, **resistance**, **patience [[transference]]** to the therapist
- **dream interpretation**/ dream work: "royal road to the unconscious"
	- rich with early childhood origin of our problems
- Interpretation of patient resistance: 
	-  Resistance refers to a patient's refusal to participate or engage in a particular topic of conversation with the therapist.
	- resistance is a **clue** to unconscious origins of psychological problems
- free association: whatever comes into patients' mind & stream of consciousness

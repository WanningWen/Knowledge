> [!important] Watson and Rayner’s (1920) infamous experiment which induce fear through associations
> lacking ethical standards, was groundbreaking in the development of learned behaviors

- In this study, Little Albert developed a fear of white rats by pairing a white rat with a loud sound.
- Over time, researchers have been able to replicate these findings (in more ethically sound ways) to provide further evidence of the role of [[respondent conditioning]] in the development of phobias

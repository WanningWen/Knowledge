> [!important] [[Schizophrenia]] [[Cognitive Behavioral Therapy|CBT]] treatment:
> focuses on the problems in thinking: attention, concentration and ​memory problems
> - complete computer based tasks ​designed to improve their cognitive abilities over time.
> - become increasingly challenging and more complex over time. ​
> - eventually, ​the computer tasks require the use of planning and ​organizational skills, as well as social and interpersonal awareness

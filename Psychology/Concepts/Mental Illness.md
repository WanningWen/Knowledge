#Career/PeerSupportSpecialist/Session 

> [!important] A mental illness is a ==brain condition== that affects a person's ==thinking, emotion or behavior== that interferes with or limits major life activities. Such conditions may affect ==someone's ability to relate to others and daily function==.

Mental Illness > Mental Health Condition > [[mental health challenge|Mental Health Challenge]]

[[Signs]] and [[Symptoms|symptoms]] of a mental health condition often appear during adolescence and young adulthood. All ages are susceptible, but youth and seniors show higher rates.

## Definitions:
#Career/BehavioralHealth/Training/YouthMHFAFirstAid

> [!important] disrupts the person's ability to 
> 	work or attend school
> 	carry out daily activities
> 	engage in satisfying relationships
Mental illnesses can affect persons of any age, race, religion, or income.

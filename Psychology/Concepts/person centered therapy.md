> [!important] The therapists responsibility is to provide [[unconditional positive regard]] toward the client, which presumably the client did not receive as a child
> 

> [!question] relationship to [[Person-Centered Care]]?

## Method:
The therapist is warm, accepting, understanding and genuine toward the client, providing the relationship and experience the client missed out on. Demonstration of unconditional positive regard towards the client, allows the client to develop the unconditional self regard that has been missing. In this way, the relationship between the therapist and client allows for psychological healing and continued growth and self actualization of the client.

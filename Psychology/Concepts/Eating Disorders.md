>[!info] Eating disorders are illnesses in which people experience severe disturbances in their eating behaviors and related thoughts and emotions

#Career/BehavioralHealth/Training/YouthMHFAFirstAid 
Young ppl w eating disorders:
- typically become preoccupied with food and their body weight
- serious and potentially life threatening
- may have [[comorbidity]] with other mental illnesses (ie. [[Anxiety Disorders]] [[depression]], [[substance use disorders]] )

## Facts:
#Career/BehavioralHealth/Training/YouthMHFAFirstAid 
- 2.3% of youth will be diagnosed with an eating disorder during their lifetime
- associated with higher lifetime risk for suicide

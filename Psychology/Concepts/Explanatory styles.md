> [!important] 
### Permanent vs. Temporary 
> [!NOTE] People who believe good events have permanent causes will be more hopeful.
#### Example:
• I did well because I am smart.
• We always have fun in our NAMI FPSS Webinar
> [!NOTE] People who believe good events have temporary causes will be more despairing.
#### Example:
• I did well because I was feeling good.
• I sometimes have fun in my NAMI class.
### Internal vs. External
- Hopeful people recognize it’s not personal (External Cause). 
- Hopeful people place “blame” for bad events on things they cannot control
### Specific vs. Universal
Hopeful people believe **good** events have **global, or universal causes.** 
Hopeful people believe **bad** events have **specific causes**. 
#### Example: 
- I had a great time because my NAMI class is fun.
- I had a bad time because this *particular* class was boring.

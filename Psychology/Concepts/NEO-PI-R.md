> [!important] a type of personality inventory test measuring the five major domains of personality – Neuroticism, Extroversion, Openness, Agreeableness, and Conscientiousness.

a concise measure of the ==five major domains of personality – **Neuroticism**, **Extroversion**, **Openness**, **Agreeableness**, and **Conscientiousness**.== 
- Six facets define each of the five domains
- the measure assesses emotional, interpersonal, experimental, attitudinal, and motivational styles (Costa & McCrae, 1992).

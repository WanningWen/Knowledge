> [!important] constant stream of negative thoughts

leads to symptoms of [[Depressive Disorders|depression]]: individuals begin to feel as though they are inadequate or helpless in a given situation.

Research studies have continually supported Beck’s maladaptive thoughts, attitudes, and errors in thinking as fundamental issues in those with depressive disorders (Lai et al., 2014; Possel & Black, 2014)

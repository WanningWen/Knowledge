## Process
1. begins with psychoeducation --> therapist help client develop coping strategies to manage traumatic memories they confront in treatment
2. ==client identifies target== (specific traumatic memory + thoughts feelings and physical sensations associated with it)
3. client ==moves their eye side to side== at the same time as calling to mind all aspects of the memory, or ==following flashing light==
4. therapist and client discuss how the experience was like
5. ending: ==calls to mind a positive belief and emotion ==while calling to mind the traumatic memory
## Criticism
scientific evidence of how it's effective in relieving symptoms of [[Post Traumatic Stress Disorder]] might have **nothing to do with the eye movement**

> [!important] ***in vivo exposure*** to traumatic experiences
> seeks to ==create narrative of traumatic experiences== to recall and verbally describe memories and images associated with their trauma ==over extended period of time==

***in vivo exposure:***
client confronting actual objects, instance, and situations associated with the trauma
- slow and gradually increasing pace
## Process:
1. Psycho education about impact of trauma on behavior thoughts emotions and the body
2. client taught breathing exercises to manage distress
3. ==detailed recall process repeatedly== with a therapist
	- each retelling of the narrative, client is less and less distressed by it -> original trauma no longer holds power against them and symptoms significantly decreas

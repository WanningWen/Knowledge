> communicate through electrical impulses that travel down the length of one neuron and picked up by another 


## Dendrites
"antennas of the neurons"
receive electrical impulse that travels through the neuron down to the end of the axon


## Axon
long fiber extending through one side of the neuron
- end has neuro link

axon -> nerve ending -> dendrites of the next neuron


## Synapse:
- electrical impulse reaches end of the axon to the synapse
- release ==[[neurotransmitter]]== into the synapse
- chemical travels across the synapse and attaches itself onto the dendrites of another neuron at ==receptor sites==
- dendrite receive message to generate electrical impulse -> travel to axon of this neuron

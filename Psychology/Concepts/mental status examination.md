> [!important] Tool used to organize the information collected during the interview and systematically evaluates the patient through a series of questions assessing appearance and behavior

# Information Including
- appearance and behavior: includes grooming and body posture
- thought processes and content: disorganized speech or thought and false beliefs
- mood and affect: whether the person feels hopeless or elated
- intellectual functioning: speech and memory
- awareness of surroundings: where the person is and what the day and time are

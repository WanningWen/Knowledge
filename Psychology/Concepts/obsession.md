> [!important] repetitive and persistent thoughts, urges, or images
> Need to be intrusive and time-consuming (take more than an hour a day), causing significant distress/impairment

Examples (Cisler, Adams, et al., 2011; Yadin & Foa, 2009)
-  contamination (dirt on self or objects)
- errors of uncertainty regarding daily behaviors (locking the door, turning off appliances)
- thoughts of physical harm or violence
- orderliness

> [!important] impact the levels of specific [[neurotransmitter]]s in the brain

Does not cure mental illness 
•Often ==significantly improve symptoms==
•May ==also help make other treatments more effective==
### Examples:
- treating [[Depressive Disorders]] by increase presence of [[serotonin]] in the brain
	- [[Antidepressant]]
- treating [[schizophrenia]] decrease [[dopamine]] activity in synapses
- [[Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors]]: 
	- in dosages also used to treat [[anxiety]]
	- reduce intense negative emotions & hyper arousal with PTSD
	- not effective to treat: intrusive memories, flashbacks, depersonalizations, derealization, avoidance behaviors

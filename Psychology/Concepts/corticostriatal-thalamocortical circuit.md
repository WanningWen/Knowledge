> [!important] theorized as a major contributor to panic symptoms (Gutman, Gorman, & Hirsch, 2004)
## Process:
 1. an individual is presented with a frightening object or situation, the [[amygdala]] is activated
 2. a fear response to the **anterior cingulate cortex** and the **orbitofrontal cortex**
 3. from the amygdala to the hypothalamus activates **endocrinologic responses** to fear
 4. releasing adrenaline and cortisol to help prepare the body to fight or flight (Gutman, Gorman, & Hirsch, 2004).

> [!important] a class of sedative-hypnotic drugs
>  originally replaced barbiturates as the leading anti-[[Generalized Anxiety Disorder|anxiety]] medication due to their less addictive nature, yet equally effective ability to calm individuals at low dosages
## Side effect:
- serious side effects, as well as physical dependence of benzodiazepines at large dosages, has routinely been documented (NIMH, 2013)

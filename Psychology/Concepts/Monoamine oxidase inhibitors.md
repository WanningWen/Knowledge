> [!important] [[Antidepressant]]
> MAOIs  prevent the monoamine oxidase from removing excess neurotransmitters norepinephrine, serotonin, and dopamine.  (Shulman, Herman & Walker, 2013).
- not typically first-line medications due to their safety concerns with hypertensive crises (Shulman, Herrman & Walker, 2013).
## Effect:
for [[Depressive Disorders|depression]] have a treatment response rate of 50-70% (Krishnan, 2007)
- the best treatment for late-stage, treatment-resistant [[Depressive Disorders|depression]] patients who have exhausted other treatment options (Krishnan, 2007).
## Side Effects:
> [!important] SERIOUS
- hypertensive episodes
- nausea
- headaches
- drowsiness
- involuntary muscle jerks
- reduced sexual desire
- weight gain
(APA, 2010)

> [!important] 
> Social psychologist Talcott Parsons's theoretical framework for patients' rights and responsibilities
## Expectations:
- _rights_:
	- to receive help from others when unwell
	- temporary reprieve of usual duties
- _responsibilities_:
	- to make efforts to improve their mental health
## Benefit:
- "blames" symptoms of [[Depressive Disorders|depression]] on the illness
- patients encouraged to receive support from:
	- family members, friends
- temporarily decrease obligations until [[Depressive Disorders|depression]] begins to improve

> [!question] when is the "sick role" not helpful:
> _In some cultures or settings (e.g., in some low-income and middle-income countries) and for some individuals, the idea of being “sick” with a mental illness is not necessarily experienced as therapeutic or non-stigmatizing._

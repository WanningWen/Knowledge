> [!important] used in [[clinical diagnosis]] for individuals with the disorder who share certain features
> “mutually exclusive and jointly exhaustive phenomenological subgroupings within a diagnosis” (APA, 2022, pg. 22).

For example:
- non-rapid eye movement (NREM) sleep arousal disorders can have either a sleepwalking or sleep terror type. 
- Enuresis is nocturnal-only, diurnal-only, or both.

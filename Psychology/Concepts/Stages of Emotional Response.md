> [!important] 
## STAGE 1 Crisis & Recuperation
> [!NOTE] Dealing with the Catastrophic Event
### Peer Characteristics:
- Dependence.
	- "After the trauma of an episode, I’m exhausted in body, mind and spirit."
- Emotions: 
	- "What is happening? "
	- "I hate myself, I’m filled with anger, resentment. "
	- "I don’t want to be with anybody."
-  Needs: 
	- "I need a safe place to rest & someone to take care of me. "
	- "I can’t take on even the simplest tasks."
### Family Members Characteristics:
- Crisis. Chaos. Shock. 
	- "Something catastrophic is going on & I don’t know now to deal with it."
- Denial. 
	- "This is not really happening. 
	- "This will pass."
	- "There’s a logical explanation.
- Hope Against Hope.
	- "If I make a huge effort everything will change & our lives will go back to normal."
## STAGE 2 Decision Making & Rebuilding
> [!NOTE] Learning to Cope While "Going through the Mill"
### Peer Characteristics:
- Rebuilding Independence.
	- I am able to do things for myself. 
	- I need help to learn & practice living skills. 
	- I feel up when I succeed & down when I don’t.
- Emotions: 
	- Hope & Grief.
	- Discouragement. Boredom. Self - doubt. 
	- Trust. Fear.
	- Excitement. Anxiety.
	- Frustration with setbacks.
	- Pride in success.
- Needs: 
	- I need to tell my story, to have others hear my pain & despair, to learn about my illness & relearn social & working skills.
	- I need people who believe in me.
### Family Members Characteristics:
- Resentment. Anger. Guilt.
	- "I’m fed up!"
	- "They need to snap out of it!"
	- "Did I cause this?"
	- "Am I too critical?"
	- "Am I too easy?"
- Recognition.
	- "Something tragic has happened & our lives are changed forever."
	- "They were a bright, vibrant person. Now they can’t even get out of bed."
- Grief.
	- "All my hopes & dreams for them are gone."
	- "I don’t know who they are." 
	- "I can’t stop crying."
	- "I feel like they died but their body is still here."
	- "I want them back."
## STAGE 3 Awakening & Recovery
> [!NOTE] Moving into Advocacy "Charge!"
### Peer Characteristics:
- Needs: 
	- I need a dream to strive for, a personal vision, someone to love, people who appreciate me, meaningful work, to advocate for myself & others.
- Emotions:
	- Self - acceptance.
	- Appreciation of others. 
	- Confidence. 
	- Anger at injustice. 
	- Assertiveness. 
	- Helpfulness to others.
- Needs: 
	- I need to tell my story, to have others hear my pain & despair, to learn about my illness & relearn social & working skills. 
	- I need people who believe in me
### Family Member Characteristics
- Understanding.
	- "I’m beginning to understand what this must be like for them."
	- "They are doing all they can."
- Acceptance.
	- "This is no one’s fault."
	- "It’s sad & difficult but we’ll hang in there & manage."
- Advocacy.
	- "We need to become a world that does not shame those with mental illness and their families."

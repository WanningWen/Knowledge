> [!important] There is past or present evidence (implicit or explicit) that an individual wishes to die, means to kill him/herself, and understands the probable consequences of his, her actions or potential actions. 
> CDC

Intent can be determined ==retrospectively== and in the ==absence of suicidal behavior==.

## 3 key elements make up intent for [[Self Directed Violence Classification System|SDVCS]]:
all three are necessary to determine intent: 
1. Evidence that the person wishes to die
2. Means to kill him/herself
3. Understands the probable consequence of his/her actions
### Ways to check for intent:
- medical records
- recent suicidal thoughts/ ideations
- contact other providers, friends, family

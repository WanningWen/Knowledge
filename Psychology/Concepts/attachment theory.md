> [!important] we are biologically wired from infancy to be in relationships for protection, safety, growth and development

## Attachment Styles:
- **Secure** 
	able to trust in and recruit help from others
- **Anxious preoccupied**
	seeks high levels of reassurance from others, has low self-esteem
- **Dismissing avoidant**
	tends to be self-sufficient, holds a negative view of others, prefers to be alone, dismisses offers of help
- **Disorganized/unresolved** 
	simultaneously desires, needs and fears others, thus unable to connect with others in times of need, with more pervasive interpersonal difficulties

> [!important] 
> occurs when a provider can pick up where another provider left off without missing a beat

For example, if you promised a family member that your agency will provide them with a list of **homeless shelters**, list it in the case notes so that the next person seeing the client knows that the family was looking for homeless shelters. This will **allow another coworker to verify that the list was provided or signal to that coworker the family still needs that resource**. For that reason, if you are the one providing the resource, **you need to document in the notes that you presented the person with a resource list of shelters**.

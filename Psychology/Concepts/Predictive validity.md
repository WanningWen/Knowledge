> [!important] *Predictive validity* is when a tool accurately predicts what will happen in the future. 

### Ex:
Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT). 
We would have high school students take it by their senior year and then wait until they are in college for a few years and see how they are doing. If they did well on the SAT, we would expect that at that point, they should be doing well in college.

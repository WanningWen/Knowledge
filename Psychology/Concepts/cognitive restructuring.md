> [!important] identify negative, automatic thoughts that contribute to the distress in social situations --> establish new, positive thoughts to replace these negative thoughts
## Method:
- encourage the patient to view thoughts as “hypotheses” as opposed to fact, which allows the beliefs to be questioned and challenged
- coupled with [[self monitoring]] -> discuss what the patient recorded for correlation between physiological arousal and thoughts/behaviors
## For [[Social Anxiety Disorder]]
> [!note]
> before, during, and after [[Exposure treatments]] sessions are effective for [[Social Anxiety Disorder]]

## How to notice distress and handling a difficult conversations
#### Recognize what's something wrong
- know what is typical of them
- "normal" mood
- notice a change
### noticing [[Major Depressive Disorder|depression]]
- lack of emotional expression
- no longer feels interested in doing things they normally enjoy doing
- significant fatigue/low energy
- speak slowly
#### Noticing [[Anxiety Disorders|Anxiety]]
- restless
#### initiating conversation
- right time place and circumstances
- state what you've notice, express you care, 
- no labels, avoid defensiveness

low insight -- not noticing there's a significant impact of someone's mental health 

ends conversation if defensive

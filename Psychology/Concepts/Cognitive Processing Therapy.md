## Process:
1. begins with ==psychoeducation==: impact of trauma on thoughts emotions and behavior
2. ==writing highly detailed narrative== of their traumatic experiences: memories and images they typically avoid --> engage in exposure process with the traumatic event 
3. Therapist helps them process the event by reflecting on dysfunctional thoughts patterns, interpretations, attitudes, beliefs, etc in written narrative account
4. work together to develop healthy adaptive thought process


can be done either individual/group

> [!important] 
> a motivational theory in psychology comprising a five-tier model of human needs, often depicted as hierarchical levels within a pyramid.

- The most fundamental needs at the base of the pyramid (adequate food, clothing and shelter)
- the middle of the pyramid (safety and supportive relationships)
- [[Self actualization]] is found at the very top of the pyramid
	- according to Maslow, self actualization can not occur unless all the needs below it are met.

## Role transitions
> [!NOTE] Changes in life roles or circumstances
### Beginning phase:
- Choose when symptoms are linked to a major change and difficulty adjusting
### Middle Phase:
 - understand: explore challenges & losses
 - promote: adapt to life changes & new social roles
## Grief
> [!NOTE] Death of a significant other
### Beginning phase:
- Choose when symptoms are linked to bereavement
### Middle Phase:
 - understand: facilitate grieving process 
 - promote: re-engage in present relationships
## Role disputes
> [!NOTE] Interpersonal disagreements
### Beginning phase:
- Choose when symptoms are linked to conflict with a significant other
### Middle Phase:
 - understand: explore the dispute & problematic communication
 - promote: 
	 - clarify & revise expectation
	 - improve communication
	 - resolve dispute
## Interpersonal sensitivity / loneliness
> [!NOTE] Chronic loneliness or difficult relationships
### Beginning phase:
- Choose when no single event triggered symptoms, but relationships are long-standing, limited, or strained
### Middle Phase:
 - understand: explore past & current relationships (including [[Rapport|therapeutic alliance]])
 - promote: increase social skills --> mitigate isolation

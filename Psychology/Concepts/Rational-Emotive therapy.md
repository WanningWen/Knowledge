> [!important] one of the first forms of [[Cognitive Behavioral Therapy|CBT]]
> by Albert Ellis in the mid-1950s: a treatment to address negative thoughts and provide relief to those suffering from [[Anxiety Disorders|anxiety]] and [[Depressive Disorders|depression]]
 
 Goal: 
-  to identify irrational, self-defeating assumptions
- challenge the rationality of those assumptions
- replace them with new, more productive thoughts and feelings. 
- experience relief of [[Generalized Anxiety Disorder|generalized anxiety disorder]] symptoms (Ellis, 2014).

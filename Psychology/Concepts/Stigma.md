> [!important] A mark of disgrace that sets a person apart from others
### Social stigma:

> [!NOTE] disapproval of, discrimination against a person based on perceivable social characteristics that serve to distinguish them from other members of a society

## Impact of Stigma & Discrimination on [[Mental Illness]]es
- Reluctance to seek help
- Lack of understanding
- Fewer opportunities
- Bullying or harassment
- Lack of confidence
- Fewer disclosures
- Family may be shunned
- Labeling
- Isolation
- Fear
- Worsening of condition
## How can you help:
### Be Available 
- Share
- Listen
- Advocate
### Be a Voice
- Stand Up
- Do Not Tolerate Stigma or Discrimination
- Plan Ahead
- Support

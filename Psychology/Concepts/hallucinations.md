> [!important] “perception-like experiences that occur without an external stimulus” (APA, 2022, pg. 102) 
> occur in any of the 5 senses:
> - hearing (auditory hallucinations)
> - seeing (visual hallucinations)
> - smelling (olfactory hallucinations)
> - touching (tactile hallucinations)
> - tasting (gustatory hallucinations)
## epidemiology/ prevalence
patients with [[Schizophrenia]]:
- half with auditory hallucinations
- 15% report visual hallucinations
- 5% report tactile hallucinations (DeLeon, Cuesta, & Peralta, 1993)
## Etiology:
people with auditory hallucinations -- [[broca's area]] is particularly active
- person is actually ​listening to their own thoughts, ​but is unable to recognize them as their own

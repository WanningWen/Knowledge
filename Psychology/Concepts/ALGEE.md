# Assess for risk of suicide or harm
>[!important] ==Approach== & ==Assess== for crisis situation, and ==assist==

![[CleanShot 2025-09-16 at 10.03.42@2x.png]]

- plan to talk privately about the concern at a convenient time in place free of distractions
- person may not open up
	- they may downplay it
	- DO NOT put pressure on them to talk right away
	- consider your role & relationship with the student
#### Helpful approaches:
- "how long have you been feeling like this"
- "have you spoken to anyone about this before?"
- "I noticed that you haven't been joining your friends at lunch -- are you doing okay?"
- "it is hard for me to understand exactly what you are going through, but I can see that it's distressing for you"
- "I noticed that something seems to be bothering you. Do you want to talk about it"
	- ==puts the question not on "something is bothering you", but if they are ready to talk about it==
	- "if you're not comfortable talking to me right now, I'm here if you would want to talk about this later"
	- not about what they've done but what I've observed

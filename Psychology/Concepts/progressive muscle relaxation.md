> [!important] tense and relax various large muscle groups
> start at head/feet, to entire body, hold tension for roughly 10 seconds before relaxing
> in tensing the muscles for a prolonged period, the individual exhausts those muscles, forcing them (and eventually) the entire body to engage in relaxation (McCallie, Blum, & Hood, 2006).

> [!important] There's a limit area we can focus on energy on
> - Circle of control（我可以控制的）
> - circle of influence （我可以影响的）
> - circle of concern (我只能关心的)
### Examples:
- putting energy on things you can only be concerned about and cannot change, you would feel less empowered

> [!important] Subclass of [[Bipolar Disorders]] (APA, 2022)
>  individuals who experience **numerous periods with hypomanic symptoms** that do not meet the criteria for a [[hypomanic episode]] and _mild_ depressive symptoms
## symptoms
- occur for two or more years
- typically interrupted by periods of **normal mood not lasting more than two months** at a time
- cause clinically significant distress or impairment in important areas of functioning
it can eventually progress into [[bipolar]] I or II disorder (Zeschel et al., 2015)

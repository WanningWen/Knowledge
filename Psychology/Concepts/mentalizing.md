> [!important] human capacity to imagine the motivations and perspectives in our own and others’ minds that underly overt behavior
> framework to understand relational problems in [[communication analysis]]
> - empathic awareness of oneself and others
> - appreciation for how one is experienced or understood by others
> - readiness to see the intention beyond and behind communications
> - reflection and perspective-taking.

## Mentalizing theory:
The experience of being accurately understood and reflected back --> a person's ability to making accurate inferences about others’ motivations --> respond effectively to adversity and challenge” and to be resilient in the face of stress
#### causes [[Depressive Disorders|depression]]
mentalizing impairments and poor reflective functioning -> depressive psychopathology
- difficulties in identifying and interpreting one’s own and others’ mental states causes an inability to recruit or use [[Interpersonal Inventory|support system]] in a state of depression

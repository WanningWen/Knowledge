## Cultural Humility
- being aware of your *own heritage*  & *value the differences* of others
- aware of your own *values & biases* and their *impact on others*
- work to:
	- strengthen comfort levels with other cultures
	- strengthen sensitivity to valuing others' perspective
- Acknowledge your attitudes & beliefs regarding people of other cultures

> [!important] cultural, social, psychological, biological factors underlie distress and resilience
## Factors:
- **[[predisposing factor]]**:
	- such as a history of past or family medical and psychiatric illness or trauma
- **[[precipitating factor]]s**
	- such as stressful life events of interpersonal losses, changes, disputes and loneliness
- **[[perpetuating factor]]s**, 
	- including social determinants of health, mental health stigma, insecure attachment and lack of social supports
- **[[protective factor]]s**
	- including social supports, culture, secure attachment and food/income/housing security.

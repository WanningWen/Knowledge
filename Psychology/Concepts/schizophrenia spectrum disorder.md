> [!NOTE] one of the following main symptoms
> - positive symptoms:
> 	- [[delusion]]
> 	- [[hallucinations]]
> - disorganized symptoms: 
> 	- disorganized thinking (speech) take ​the form of **inappropriate affect (emotions)**
> 		- [[circumstantial speech pattern]] vs [[tangential speech pattern]]
> 		- [[speech incoherence]]
> 		- [[derailment|thought derailment]]
> 		- [[illogicality]]
> 	- disorganized or abnormal motor behavior
> 		- [[catatonic behavior]]
> - [[negative symptoms]]
> 	- **Diminished emotional expression –** Reduction in emotional expression; reduced display of emotional expression
>	- **[[alogia]]** – Poverty of speech or speech content
>	- **[[anhedonia]]** – Inability to experience pleasure
>	- **Asociality** – Lack of interest in social relationships
>	- **[[avolition]] –** Lack of motivation for goal-directed behavior

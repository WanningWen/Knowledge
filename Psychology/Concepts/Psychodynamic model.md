## Successors from Freud:
Both pursued study of aspects of human experience that are decidedly more positive than anything Freud ever talked about. 
### Alfred Adler
- interested in how individual people contributed to the well being of other individuals and to society as a whole
### Carl Jung
- very interested in understanding goal setting and achievement. 
These two theorists never turned fully away from [[psychoanalytic theory]]. Nonetheless, the positive aspects of their theories led to the development of the [[humanistic model]].

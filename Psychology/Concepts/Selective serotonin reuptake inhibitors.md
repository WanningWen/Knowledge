> [!NOTE] improve [[depression]] symptoms by blocking the reuptake of norepinephrine and/or [[serotonin]] in presynaptic [[neurons]]  to increase those received by postsynaptic [[neurons]]
## Illnesses:
- [[Post Traumatic Stress Disorder]]
- [[Anxiety Disorders]]
## Effect:
- also used to treat anxiety
- reduce intense negative emotions & hyper arousal with PTSD
## not effective to treat: 
- intrusive memories
- flashbacks
- depersonalizations
- derealization
- avoidance behaviors

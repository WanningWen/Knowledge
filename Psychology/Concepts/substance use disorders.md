>[!info] A **substance use disorder** is 
>a diagnosable pattern of using a substance that disrupts a young person's ability to work or go to school, engage in satisfying relationships, or participate in their usual daily activities

#Career/BehavioralHealth/Training/YouthMHFAFirstAid 
Alcohol, cannabis, and tobacco are substance used by adolescents

### Quick Facts:
- 10.2% of youth will be diagnosed in their lifetime
- [[comorbidity]] with other [[mental health challenge]]s to manage painful or difficult feelings

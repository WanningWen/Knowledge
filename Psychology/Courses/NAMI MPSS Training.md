## Session 6- Sharing Your Story Part 2 and Recovery Model
### Seven Steps to Sharing Your Story 
1. Introduce Yourself!
2. Share a little about you as a Peer with lived experience or family member and getting through a difficult period
3. What helped during that period?
4. How are you different today?
5. What is your listener’s need or problem?
6. What ideas/resources can you offer?
7. Leave them with hope that they will get through it.

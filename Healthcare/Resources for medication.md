## Better Health Pharmacy

[Español](https://publichealth.santaclaracounty.gov/programs-and-services/pharmacy-and-vaccination-services/better-health-pharmacy/farmacia-better "Farmacia Better Health")[Tiếng Việt](https://publichealth.santaclaracounty.gov/programs-and-services/pharmacy-and-vaccination-services/better-health-pharmacy/nha-thuoc-better "Nhà thuốc Better Health")

Better Health Pharmacy is the first and only dedicated drug donation pharmacy in California. We receive unused, unopened, and unexpired medications from licensed healthcare facilities, and dispense the drugs at no cost to our patients who have a valid prescription. 

Our pharmacy aims to increase the access to medication for everyone and serve patients who cannot afford their medications.

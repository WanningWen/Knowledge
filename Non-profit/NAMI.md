### The StigmaFree campaign
NAMI’s effort to end stigma and create hope for those affected by mental illness. Through powerful words and actions, we can shift the social and systemic barriers for those living with mental health conditions. Together, we can encourage acceptance and understanding. Together, we can advocate for a better world. Together, we can turn StigmaFreeMe into StigmaFreeWe

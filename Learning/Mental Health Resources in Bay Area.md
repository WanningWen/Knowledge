## If you’re not sure what to do
- Crisis hotlines / text lines / county call centers are always OK to use for guidance.
- Mental Health & Suicide Prevention Resources

## National
### Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
988 (language translation available)
1-800-273-8255 / 1-800-SUICIDE
### Crisis Text Line (English only)
Text HOME to 741741
### Asian LifeNet Hotline (culturally tailored)
1-877-990-8585 (24/7)
Languages: Cantonese, Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, Fujianese

## Behavioral health information

> [!important] philosopher (1623 - 62)
- bleak view of humanity
	- evidence of [[the Fall]]
	- similar to [[Augustine]], believe human beings are drive by sexual desire and are unreliable
- insignificance of our lives in relation to the eternity before and after our lives
- humanity has potential if we don't lose sight of God

## Works:
### *Pensées* ('Thoughts')
- a defense of Pascal's version of Christianity
#### [[Jansenism]]
![[Jansenism]]
- believed that belief in God was about the heart and faith, not reasoning (unlike [[René Descartes]])
#### Pascal's Wager
![[Pascal's Wager]]
##### How does a non-believer get to have faith in God?

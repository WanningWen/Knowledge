> [!important] originally Jewish Philosopher from the Netherlands (Amsterdam)
> believed in [[pantheism]]
- Similar to [[René Descartes]] and [[Thomas Hobbes]], was inspired to use mathematical deduction. Spinoza used geometrical reasoning and proofs

## Works:
### *Ethics*
- wrote philosophy with axioms and definitions
- used relentless logic similar to geometry about God, nature, freedom, and emotion
- ended sections with 'QED' ("*quod erat demonstrandum*") meaning "which was to be proved"

His approach to philosophy was often labelled as [[Rationalism]]
- discussed philosophy ideas with Gottfried Leibniz
### Idea of God:
- if God is infinite, there cannot be anything that is not God
- traditional religion believed God responds to preaching [[anthropomorphism]]

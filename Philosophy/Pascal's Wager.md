> [!important] [[Blaise Pascal]]'s argument to persuade those who are unsure whether or not God exists or they should believe in God based in [[Probability]]
> "If you win you win everything; if you lose you lose nothing"
> If chosen to believe in God, but it turns out incorrect, you won't have made a substantial sacrifice except in 'those poisonous pleasures'
- [[Blaise Pascal]] believed that no rational person would do anything else but gamble on God existing
### Argument against:
1. For many non-religious, devoting their lives to a religion for the potential of living in illusion would *be* a sacrifice
2. To believe in God just because the odds are favorable seems self-interested
3. What if the Christian God is the "Wrong god"?

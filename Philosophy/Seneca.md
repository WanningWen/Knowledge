## Argued against brevity of life
**Did not agree** with Ancient Greek Hippocrates stated "Life is short, art is long"
- old people fear death because they wish for a few more years to complete achievements
His counter:
- life is long enough if we make the right choices
- benefit: you won't face the pain of contemplating all the missed opportunities
### What is truly living:
- living like a recluse away from other people

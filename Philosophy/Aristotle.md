Unlike [[Plato]] and [[Socrates]] who focus on the world we see as a pale reflection of true reality only reachable by philosophical thought, Aristotle was fascinated by details of everything around him
- The way to understand any general category is to examine particular examples
## *"The Nicomachean Ethics"* 
"One swallow doesn't make a summer"
- a few moments of pleasure don't add up to true [[Happiness|happiness]]
## How should we live? Seek [[Happiness]]
![[Happiness]]
### *Eudaimonia*: flourishing/ success
humans are different than animals because of our ability to reason
- therefore, best kind of life for a human is one which used our powers of reason
#### How to achieve more *Eudaimonia*
- develop the right kind of character - "Good patterns of behavior are virtues; bad ones vices"
- only achieved **in relation to life in a society**

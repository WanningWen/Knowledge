> [!important]  don't accept anything as true if there is the slightest possibility that it isn't
> Take a belief -- examine it -- only accept if you're certain it cannot be wrong or misleading -- reject if there's tiniest room for doubt
### Example process:
- unwise to trust senses because it can trick you (has tricked you in the past)
- How do you know you aren't dreaming now -- you *might* be dreaming -- reject I am awake
- mathematical truths (ex: 2+3 = 5): demon can trick you
#### Evil demon thought experiment:
There's no way of knowing what we are doing for certain because it might be an illusion by a demon
### "I think, therefore I am" '*cogito ergo sum*'
> [!NOTE] as long as we have a thought **at all**, we must exist
- it is impossible to doubt that you exist, what you are is another question

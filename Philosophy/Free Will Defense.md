> [!important] Theodicy to explain the [[Problem of Evil]] : God given free will for human beings to choose what we will do next

[[Augustine]] believed that having free will allows us to choose to be moral/good
- choose to follow God's commands (the Ten Commandments, "Love thy neighbor")
- There's no point in thinking of how to behave if God were to pull all the strings
However, we develop strong desires for things and our emotions overpower reason

> [!important] The idea from philosopher [[René Descartes]] that your mind is separate from the body and interacts with it 
> Common idea amongst religious people. Basis that the mind/spirit lives on after death of the body

20th century philosopher Gilbert Ryle mocked this as "the myth of the ghost in the machine" - the body was the machine and the soul is the ghost inhabiting it

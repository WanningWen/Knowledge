Problems:
- our family is everything: 
	- hard to distinguish themselves from families
	- end up resentful
	- cannot step away from unhealthy relationships
	- parents think children's decisions reflect on their family
	- "how much to negotiate with parents"
		- what is their own decision, what is parents'
- perfectionism "never good enough" critical parents -> inner voice
	- comparison prevents us from genuinely enjoying relationships
- "being different and looking different"
- security + stability (risk-averse) afraid to try new things
	- limit career, dating
	- unable to try something new unless we can do it right the first time
- interrogated on choices that are different than their parents
	- hiding details to avoid conflict
- "perform" for the appreciation
- being afraid of looking vulnerable and not seek help
	- could leave them in places they don't enjoy for long periods of time

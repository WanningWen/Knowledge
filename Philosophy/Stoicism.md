> [!important] 
>  we are responsible for what we feel and think
>  emotions cloud reasoning and damage judgement
### **etymology**:
from the *Stoa*: painted porch in Athens where these philosophers met
## Connection to [[psychotherapy]]:
Promoted a state of indifference (calming emotions) which may reduce unhappiness in the face of events we can't control

Counter, *however*:
- may become cold, heartless, less human
# Philosophers:
## Zeno of Citium (334 - 262 BC)
cared about philosophical problems of reality, logic and ethics
- we should only worry about things we can change
- similar to [[Pyrrho]]'s Skeptics, aiming for a calm state of mind
> [!NOTE] Our *attitude* to events is within our control even though *what* happens isn't

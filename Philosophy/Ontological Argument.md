> [!important] 
> The fact that we have an idea of God proves that God actually exists by [[Anselm]]
## Logic:
1. God is the being which "nothing greater than can be conceived"  -- greatest being imaginable
2. we can clearly have an idea of God -- God could *conceivably* exist
3. the imagined God cannot be greater than an existing one
4. [[Anselm]] concluded God *must* exist simply from the fact that we have an idea of God
